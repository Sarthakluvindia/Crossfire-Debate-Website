RULES FOR THE DEBATE

There are certain rules for the debate and all the participants are expected to follow them. These rules are for the round one:

OPENING SPEECH

The opening speech would be of 1.5 minutes each, given first by the proposition followed by the opposition.

REBUTTAL ROUND

The rebuttal round will be of 4 minutes. The proposition will ask the question first followed by the opposition. There will be no cross questioning in this round.

CLOSING SPEECH

The closing speech would be of 1.5 minutes each, given first by the opposition followed by the proposition.

WARNING BELL

A warning bell would be rung 30 seconds prior to the stipulated time. The contestant may ask the time keeper to change the time.
