Why Are We Different?

Sometimes stories are said in whispers. Sometimes ideas are scribbled away on the last page. Sometimes what needs to be heard is taken for granted. If all it took was an ear and an eye to bring the intangible to reality, then we'd have never run out of things to do. That's why we make the first step. We present a compilation of ideas and stories meant to evoke one distinct feeling, inspiration.

We have successfully conducted some great event in the college which have received public acclaim. Our events like SudoCross, Open Mic Night, Intra-College Crossfire Debate and Blind Date With A Book have been a great success.

Editorial Board

Editorial Board is a group of people looking to bring out the best in others. We bring stories to life, by expressing perspective without restraint, something of which we are really proud of.
